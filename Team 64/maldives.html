Top places and tourist attraction to visit in Maldives



BIYADHOO BEACH


A ring-shaped island resort resting in the South Male Atoll, Biyadhoo is mostly eclipsed with some rich verdure and umber-brown forests. Biyadhoo is home to a diversity of fruits and vegetation including mangoes and coconuts, cucumbers, tomatoes and cabbages. Oftentimes, referred to as the Scuba Diving Island-Biyadhoo Beach is marked famous for being home to the site of Kandooma Thila which in turn is held notable for soft coral and a varied species of fishes. Encased by the majestic Indian Ocean, the island of Biyadhoo offers a striking view of the Maldives
